### 🧠 About me

I'm a Data Scientist and ML/AI Engineer from Argentina, and co-founder of **Quanta**, an AI & automation consultancy. I lead projects end to end — from discovery and data architecture to deployed, production-grade ML systems and SaaS products that real businesses use every day.

I work across three verticals where I've delivered measurable impact:

- 💰 **Finance** — debt-consolidation and financial-intelligence apps with forecasting, anomaly detection and risk scoring.
- 🌱 **Agro-industry** — analytics, ETL pipelines and predictive modules for large agro-industrial operations.
- 👥 **Human Resources** — data consolidation, analytics and ML-powered applications.

What I actually do:

- 🚀 **Launch products & services** — from idea to live SaaS, owning the full lifecycle.
- 🧱 **Build MLOps systems** — reproducible pipelines, model tracking, deployment and monitoring.
- 🤖 **Implement AI** — LLM-powered agents, automation workflows and decision-support tools embedded in real operations.
- 📊 **Bridge tech & business** — I write board-level documentation in plain language so non-technical leaders can act on the data.
